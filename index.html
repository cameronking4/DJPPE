Our Mission


The Duke University Journal of Philosophy, Politics, and Economics is a peer-reviewed, online journal publishing works of philosophy, politics, and economics by undergraduate and graduate students from around the world. Sponsored by the Duke PPE Certificate program, the Duke JPPE hopes to publish original scholarship that offers a cross-disciplinary perspective. In keeping with the goal of the PPE discipline, this publication will feature a wide-range of important issues and highlight the dynamic relationship between the political, economic, and philosophical orders.
